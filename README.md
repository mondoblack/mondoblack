# Mondoblack

A prescient science-fiction short story I published in 2019. 

## Summary

In a future dominated by AI and universal basic income, humans seek purpose and
meaning through co-operative networks and artistic expression, grappling with
feelings of obsolescence and the unsettling nature of advanced technology like
Mondoblack, ultimately finding solace and renewed purpose in human
connection and family. The story explores the human response to a world where AI
has taken over most jobs, leading to a search for meaning and fulfillment
outside of traditional work.
